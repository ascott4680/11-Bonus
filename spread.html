Comparing Avocado Type Price Spread Phoenix

In the following visualization I wanted to see the difference in volume and price for conventional and organic avocados.
  • Conventional avocados outsell organic avocados by nearly 2x
  • Prices for conventional avocados range from $0.45 to $1.35 ea.
  • Prices for organic avocados range from $1.20 to $2.60 ea.



Avocado Fact- Avocados are a fruit, not a vegetable. They belong to the Genus Persea in the Lauraceae family.

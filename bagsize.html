Average Price Comparison

In the following visualization I wanted to see if there was any correlation on bag size of conventional avocados to price. In the results below we can draw the following conclusions:
  • No correlation between price and bag size
  • The volume of Xtra Large bags is very low when compared to small and large bags
  • As the volume of avocados sold increases the price stays below $0.80 per avocado
